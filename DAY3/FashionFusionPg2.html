My Favorite Styles.

FASHION FUSION

#1 "Early 2000s Punk Rock"

This style has earned #1 in my heart because as someone who isn't really heavely effected by media. I watched a Japanese anime by the name of "Nana". One of the main characters was in a band with her friends and I thought the way they dressed was super cool. Their styles were mainly made of of Vivienne Westwood products and I would always see that Saturn logo everywhere. One of the characters named "Ren Honjo" was based off of Sid Vicious from the Sex Pistols which was a famous punk band in real life, which I thought was extremely cool. It seemed like the Writer Ai Yazawa was a fan, but the anime never got finished because she fell ill.

Artist: Ai Yazawa | Series: NANA

#2 "Elegant/Classy With an Edge"

After wathcing "Nana" I became interesting in another Japanese anime by the same writer/artist called "Paradise Kiss" or "ParaKiss" Which is what they call it in Japan. The story follows a young woman in highschool who is working on building her life when she comes across a group of university fashion designers who ask her to model for their fashion show and eventually begins to persue her new found dream of becoming a model. This Anime was very inspiring for me because it made me realize that I wanted to be a fashion designer when I become older. This anime takes place in the early 2000s aswell and I just fell inlove love with the Elegant and the class of the outfits that the characters would wear and how the writer/artist gave them her own edgy vibe because Ai Yazawa was such a huge punk rock fan.

Artist: Ai Yazawa | Series: NANA
